Scenarios of high-quality life for elders.

Carol, 62 years would:

  • enjoy and visit outdoor spaces

  • have a say in local a airs

  • have flexible employment opportunities

  • enjoy volunteering and sharing her skills

  • feel safe and secure in housing

Tran, 85, widowed,  would:

  • have somewhere to go and things to do

  • have an understanding of and

  • information on community events

  • access weekly tai chi lessons for a well-being

  • feel included and valued in her local community

Bettina and Steve in their 70’s would:

  • be encouraged and motivated to stay active

  • feel respected and valued by the community

  • have a say in decisions that affect their life

  • participate and socialise with people of all ages

Laci, 75 years, would:

  • have a choice of activities to join

  • have access to community transport if required

  • enjoy and feel safe in the parks and walking tracks

  • have access to support services if needed

  • have opportunities for socialisation
